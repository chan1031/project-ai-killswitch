Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Simple websites typically take 4-6 weeks, while complex applications can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. This includes updates, security patches, and technical support.

What is your development process? +

We follow an agile development methodology with regular milestones and client feedback sessions. This ensures transparency and allows for adjustments throughout the development process.

Can you work with our existing systems? +

Absolutely! We have extensive experience integrating with existing systems and can work with your current infrastructure to minimize disruption and maximize efficiency.

What payment terms do you offer? +

We typically work with milestone-based payments, with an initial deposit followed by payments at key project milestones. We're flexible and can discuss payment terms that work for your budget.
